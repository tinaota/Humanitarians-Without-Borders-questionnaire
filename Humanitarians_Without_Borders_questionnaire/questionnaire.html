UX Research — Domain Owner Survey

Website Stakeholder
Questionnaire

Help us understand the website's goals and the communities it serves — so we can improve the experience for everyone who visits.

⏱ 10–15 minutes 🔒 Responses are confidential 📋 15 questions

Section A
Website Purpose & Goals
1
What is the primary job of this website right now?
Select all that apply
2
If a visitor only does ONE thing after visiting the site, what would you want that to be?
3
Is the website currently achieving that goal? How do you know?
e.g. phone calls received, messages, donations, word of mouth

Section B
Target Audience
4
Who do you believe visits the website most?
Select all that apply
5
Are the families you serve likely to use the website themselves to find help?
This shapes how much we should optimise the site for that audience
📝
Section C
Content & Clarity
6
Is there anything important about the organisation that the website currently does NOT communicate clearly?
7
The contact section only shows a phone number and address — no email or contact form. Has this caused any problems receiving enquiries?
8
Are there any services or programs the site lists that are no longer active — or new ones that are missing?
🗺️
Section D
User Journeys & Pain Points
9
When someone calls you after visiting the website, what question do they usually ask first?
This reveals what the site failed to answer
10
Have you ever received feedback — positive or negative — about the website from a visitor, partner, or community member?
⭐
Section E
Priorities for Improvement
11
Which improvements feel most urgent to you?
Select your top 3
12
Is there a website from another organisation — in any sector — that you admire or think does a great job?
If so, which one and why? This helps us understand your design benchmark.
⚙️
Section F
Technical & Operational
13
Who currently manages the website, and how easy is it to update content when something changes?
14
Do you have access to any website analytics?
e.g. how many people visit, what pages they look at
🌱
Wrap-Up
Looking Ahead
15
What does success look like for this website in 12 months?
Dream big — what would you love to see happen as a result of improving the site?
